Skills

Ability to work alone or with a team.

I demonstrate excellent teamwork skills and have gained experience doing so in a couple different aspects of my life. At my current job, I work with a team of at least 3 others constantly. We work together as a team to make sure the department runs smoothly by doing our own separate roles while also helping each other when needed. I also ususally start working at an earlier time than others due to my role as a baker, and I am able to self-govern and complete my tasks alone. Because my role requires more hands on training than others, I need to be able to be self-sufficient when necessary. I am able to do many different tasks in my department because I have been working there for a long time, so I am able to help with regular clerk duties, management duties, and cake decorating aside from my regular duties as a baker.
