Goals

5 short term goals I have and hope to accomplish in the next two years:
  1. Completing my current program at NSCC in good academic standing with a good GPA.
  2. keep modifying my website as I learn new things throughout my education to make it more professional.
  3. Work on some LinkedIn Learning courses that interest me.
  4. Attend a networking event so I can meet people in the industry.
  5. Practice mindfulness and develop good strategies for coping with stress.

Here is how I plan to achieve these goals:

Goal 1
I plan to achieve this by getting into a good studying routine, reviewing regularly, reaching out to my instructors for help
and advice. I also plan to make good use of the resources available to me as a student, such as peer tutoring, the accessability center and a private tutor.
Goal 2
I plan to achieve this by keeping a separate folder on my computer for easy access, and I have created my own repository on
GitHub so I can keep track of my project. As I learn more programming languages, I will be able to add new features and designs
to my website, that way when I graduate and look for a job, I will be able to tailor my website to fit the job while
showcasing my abilities.
Goal 3
All NSCC students have access to LinkedIn Learning for free, so I am able to access any courses or certifications on the website.
This will be useful for professional development and gaining skills while working on current skills.
Goal 4
I would like to attend at least one event to network with others in the industry and put myself out there.
I have never gone to an event like that before but I know it will be very useful for when I graduate.
Goal 5
Balancing school, work, and home life can be stressful and difficult at times. I want to get better at practicing mindfulness because it has many benefits to our mental
and physical well-being. One of my favourite instructors began each class with a brief meditation exercise and it has been a great tool for taking a moment when needed.
We need to take care of ourselves so we can take care of our responsibilities, and this is one way to help with that.
